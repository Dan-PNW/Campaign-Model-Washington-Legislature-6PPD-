Rep. Jake Fey D-27 ENVI member — watch
Tacoma / Pierce County. Same district as Speaker Jinkins (two House seats per district). Long-serving D with a transportation and infrastructure lens. Not a co-sponsor of HB 2421. Fee-cautious: the original $3/$6 fee structure landed awkwardly in a district with significant trucking and freight economy. The stormwater investment frame (AWC's argument) is the right door — position the fee as protecting Tacoma's existing stormwater infrastructure investment, not adding a new burden. Unlikely to be a hard no; more likely needs a politically defensible frame. If he's wavering, a direct ask from AWC's Schroeder is the right move.

Rep. Sharon Wylie D-49 ENVI member — watch
Vancouver / Clark County (SW WA). Longest-serving current House member; business-friendly D. Clark County is more conservative territory — her votes track a moderate, business-conscious lens. Confidence levels: high (±5 pts) = clear on-record signals. Medium (±12 pts) = party + district + committee. Low (±18 pts) = limited public signal; treat the range seriously, not just the midpoint. The ranges are honest uncertainty bands, not statistical confidence intervals.
